About Me

Explains the chapters in my life

Growing up in Dubai as an Indian, I was fortunate to experience a rich blend of cultures and opportunities that shaped my identity and interests. I attended GEMS Winchester School, Dubai, where I developed a strong academic foundation and nurtured my curiosity. At the age of 10, I discovered my fascination with computers—not just using them, but understanding how they worked. I would spend hours disassembling devices, fixing components, and exploring their inner mechanics. This hands-on curiosity evolved into a deep passion for computer science and problem-solving.

Today, I’m pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science at the University of Limerick, where I continue to explore the endless possibilities of technology. My academic journey is driven by a desire to innovate and create meaningful solutions. Outside of academics, I find balance through my hobbies. Tennis is a major part of my life—it’s more than a sport; it’s a way to manage stress, stay fit, and sharpen focus. The discipline and mental clarity it provides have helped me in both personal and academic challenges. I also play the piano, often learning new pieces in my free time. Music allows me to express myself creatively and unwind after long days.

Another deep interest of mine is astrophysics. The mysteries of the universe captivate me, and I dream of combining this passion with computer science to develop innovative software that could revolutionize space exploration, data analysis, or simulation of cosmic phenomena. I believe that merging these two fields can lead to breakthroughs that help us better understand the universe and our place within it.
