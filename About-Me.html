About Me

Explains the chapters in my life

Growing up in Dubai as an Indian, I was fortunate to be immersed in a vibrant, multicultural environment that shaped my curiosity and creativity. At the age of 10, I discovered my fascination with computers. I wasn’t just interested in using them—I wanted to understand how they worked. I’d spend hours disassembling devices, fixing broken parts, and exploring their inner workings. That hands-on curiosity has stayed with me, evolving into a deep passion for computer science and problem-solving.

Beyond technology, I’ve always found balance through my hobbies. Tennis is one of my greatest loves. It’s not just a sport—it’s a mental escape. The focus, discipline, and physical exertion help me manage stress and stay grounded. It’s taught me resilience and the importance of staying calm under pressure. Music is another outlet for expression. I play the piano and often learn new pieces in my free time. It’s a peaceful, creative pursuit that complements my analytical side.

Academically, I’m deeply intrigued by astrophysics. The vastness of the universe and the mysteries it holds inspire me to think beyond boundaries. My dream is to combine computer science and astrophysics to develop innovative software that could transform how we explore space, analyze cosmic data, or simulate celestial phenomena. I believe that by merging these two fields, I can contribute to discoveries that push humanity forward.
